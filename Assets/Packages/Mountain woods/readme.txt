This package contains two free scripts/components from the asset store. You will need to add these to your project for the terrain to look correct. 

Terrain toolkit:
https://code.google.com/p/unityterraintoolkit/downloads/detail?name=Terrain%20Toolkit%201.0.2.zip&can=2&q=

and

ATS Colormap Terrain Shader
http://u3d.as/content/forst/ats-colormap-terrain-shader/3fr

The terrain asset comes with a hand painted detail map.  If you want to paint your own, just replace the splat map.
